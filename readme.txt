<h2>System Features</h2>
<p>
1. Tenant
  <ul>
  <li>Can register and log in to our system</li>
  <li>Can view or edit profile</li>
  <li>Can reset password</li> 
  <li>Can view available room posted by hostel owner</li>
  <li>Can filter based on hostel category</li>
  <li>Can search hostel based on hostel’s name</li>  
  <li>Can add the hostel into favourite list and cart</li>
  <li>Can make an appointment to visit the hostel</li>
  <li>Can make rental payment of first month</li>  
  <li>Can view booked house details and booking status</li>
  <li>Can do rating after they rent the hostel</li>
  <li>Can logout</li>
</ul> 
<br>
2. Hostel Owner
  <ul>
  <li>Can register and log in to our system</li>
  <li>Can view and edit profile</li>
  <li>Can post the hostel details and delete the post</li>  
  <li>Can view tenant details who have booked their house</li>
  <li>Can view tenant’s payment status</li>
  <li>Can view booking status</li>  
  <li>Can delete account</li>
  <li>Can reset password</li>
  <li>Can logout</li>  

</ul>   
3. Administrator
  <ul>
  <li>Can log in to system</li>
  <li>Can add new admin</li>
  <li>Can add coupon for tenant to have discount during payment</li>
  <li>Can view appointment which made by tenant</li>
  <li>Can approve tenant and owner registration</li>
  <li>Can view and edit own profile</li>  
  <li>Can view list of admin</li>
  <li>Can view list of tenants</li>
  <li>Can view list of hostel owners</li>  
  <li>Can view list of hostel posts</li>
  <li>Can delete tenant or owner account</li>
  <li>Can view hostel booking records</li>  
  <li>Can reset password</li>
  <li>Can view the hostel rating</li>
  <li>Can check tenant payment status</li>  
  <li>Can logout</li>
</ul>
